This is a map of the publicly released NYC Covid data. You can find the original data HERE.

The data goes back for the past 30 days and updates automatically with the data source.

Faint light green sections of the map are major parks such as Central Park or Prospect Park.

The application is in its alpha release, so some features are not perfect. Percentages and Rates are x10 in order to avoid decimals. This means 150 is 15.0%.
